Tuberculosis

Symptoms and Cause

Although your body can harbor the bacteria that cause tuberculosis, Including the kidneys, spine or brain. When TB occurs outside your lungs, signs and symptoms vary according to the organs involved. For example, tuberculosis of the spine might cause back pain, and tuberculosis in your kidneys might cause blood in your urine.

Precautions and Treatments

If you test positive for latent TB infection, your doctor might advise you to take medications to reduce your risk of developing active tuberculosis. Only active TB is contagious. If you have active TB, it generally takes a few weeks of treatment with TB medications before you're not contagious anymore.
