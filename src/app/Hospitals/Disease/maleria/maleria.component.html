Malaria

Symptoms and Cause

Malaria is a disease caused by a parasite. The parasite is spread to humans through the bites of infected mosquitoes. People who have malaria usually feel very sick with a high fever and shaking chills. Some people who have malaria experience cycles of malaria "attacks." An attack usually starts with shivering and chills, Malaria signs and symptoms typically begin within a few weeks after being bitten by an infected mosquito
